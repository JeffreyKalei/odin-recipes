Chicken

A very delicious and filling chicken recipe

10 minute ingredient prep time

10 minute cook time

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1.5 teaspoons of red pepper flakes
  • 1.5 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless, chicken breasts cut into bite-sized pieces

Instructions

  1. Season chicken and cover it with a bit of flour
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red-pepper flakes into a bowl
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, cook and stir chicken until lightly brown for about 5 minutes
  4. Pour honey mixture into skillet and continue to stir until the chicken cooks and the sauce thickens
  5. Enjoy!
