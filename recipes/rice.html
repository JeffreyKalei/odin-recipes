Rice

A very delivious and filling rice recipe

30 minute ingredient prep time

1 hour cook time

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4 eggs (beaten)
  • 2 cups of leftover rice
  • 1 bell Pepper
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • pilau masala
  • salt
  • curry powder
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Set two cups of leftover rice
  2. Cut chicken breasts into cubes, suatee and store
  3. Scramble eggs in the same pan used to cook
  4. Sautee onions and bell peppers
  5. Add spices to taste
  6. Add rice to fry
  7. Add eggs
  8. Enjoy!
